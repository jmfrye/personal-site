Street Pong, CMU, Spring 2015

Seat Planning Optimizer (SPO), APT, Summer 2015

We made an office seating arrangement planning tool during Hack & Learn 7 at Applied Predictive Technologies (Summer 2015)!

This tool helps HR decide where the business consultants should sit in the office. It accounts for their client-time allocations, office room sizes, project priorities, and current seat locations.

Exception Tagging, APT, Summer 2015

Users categorize and describe exceptions they receive, building a knowledge base for resolving previously-encountered issues.

Specifically, this feature labels exception emails and digest pages with color-coded tags containing links to detail pages that describe each exception.

Research

Smart Residential Energy Scheduling Utilizing Two Stage Mixed Integer Linear Programming

In this paper, we design and evaluate the feasibility of a system which minimizes residential electricity cost of individual homes by shifting demand over a daily forecast price cycle.
